Mission Statement

The main goals of Ubucon 2016 are:

  • Become a place where the members of the european Ubuntu communities can meet, learn form each other, exchange ideas and hopefully create friendships
  • Create a showcase for everything (flavors, commercial products etc.) based on Ubuntu
  • Inform the general public about Ubuntu and all its flavors and subprojects
  • To be as open for everybody and anybody
  • To be as much fun as possible

What Ubucon Europe 2016 is not:

  • A “generic” event like LinuxTag, OpenRheinRuhr etc.
