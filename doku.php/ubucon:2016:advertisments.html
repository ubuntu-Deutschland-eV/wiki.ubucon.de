Advertisement on Websites

In the past years this topic was limited to german-speaking websites, where important news (e.g. Call for Paper, end of the Call for Papers etc.) were posted:

  • Pro-Linux
  • Heise open
  • Linux-Magazin

There are no existing contacts to international websites

Ikhaya

Ikhaya is the News-blog of the german Ubuntu users community website. In past years, Ubucon news were summarized for Ikhaya. Regular updates were published.

Planets

The Ubucon-Feed was registered with the following planets:

  • Planet ubuntuusers.de
  • Open Source Blog Netzwerk (registered by Dee)

Mailing lists

The most important news were posted to the following lists:

  • ubuntu-de-events
  • ubucon-de
  • loco-de

There was no „official“ Ubucon sender address, so individual accounts were used.

Poster etc.

See the Launchpad project for past posters, business cards etc.:

* https://code.launchpad.net/~ubuntu-de-locoteam/+junk/loco-de
